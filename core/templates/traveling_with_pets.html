BRING YOUR FURRY FRIEND

Your pet is family—and family travels together. We'll guide you through bringing your companion to Rwanda, from documentation to pet-friendly stays.

Yes, You Can Travel to Rwanda with Your Pet!

Many travelers don't realize Rwanda welcomes well-prepared pet owners. While there are specific requirements and planning involved, bringing your dog or cat to experience Rwanda with you is absolutely possible—and we make it straightforward.

Whether relocating, traveling long-term, or simply can't imagine exploring without your companion, we'll walk you through the entire process and ensure both you and your pet have a comfortable, safe journey.

Getting Started

📋 Essential Documentation

Start this process 60-90 days before departure. Rushing these documents creates unnecessary stress for both you and your pet.

  • Valid rabies vaccination (30+ days before travel)
  • International Health Certificate from veterinarian
  • Import permit from Rwanda Agriculture Board
  • ISO-standard microchip (11784/11785 compliant)
  • Proof of parasite treatments
  • Complete vaccination records

✈️ Flight Arrangements

Not all airlines have the same pet policies. We'll help you choose the best option for your pet's size, temperament, and travel dates.

  • Small pets (under 8kg) can usually travel in-cabin
  • Larger pets travel as cargo in climate-controlled holds
  • Book pet space when booking your ticket
  • Direct flights are always better
  • IATA-approved carriers mandatory
  • Some airlines restrict breeds—check early

💡 Pro Tip: Start Early, Travel Easy

The most common mistake? Starting too late. Permits can take 4-6 weeks to process, and some vaccinations require waiting periods. Contact us 90 days before departure for a smooth, stress-free timeline.

Pet-Friendly Rwanda

🏨

Pet-Friendly Stays

Several properties in Kigali and Lake Kivu welcome pets with advance notice. We coordinate directly with hotels to ensure your companion is truly welcome.

🏥

Veterinary Care

Kigali has excellent vet clinics with English-speaking staff. Common medications readily available, emergency services accessible.

🚗

Ground Transport

Our drivers accommodate pets with proper carriers or harnesses. Regular stops every 2 hours for water, bathroom breaks, and stretching.

📦 Packing Checklist for Your Pet

  • 7-10 days of regular food
  • Collapsible food/water bowls
  • Favorite toys and blanket
  • Leash, harness, spare collar
  • Current medications (full supply)
  • Vaccination records (copies)
  • First aid kit for pets
  • Waste bags and cleaning supplies
  • Recent photo of your pet
  • Veterinarian contact info
  • Pet carrier/crate
  • Calming aids if needed

    • ⚠️ Important Restrictions

    For wildlife conservation and safety, pets are not permitted inside national parks (Volcanoes, Nyungwe, Akagera). This is strictly enforced.

    However, many lodges near parks offer pet-sitting services, or we arrange boarding at trusted facilities while you visit parks. Your pet will be safe and well-cared for.

    🌡️ Climate & Altitude

    Kigali sits at 1,500m elevation. Most pets adjust within 2-3 days. The mild climate (15-27°C year-round) is comfortable.

    Increase water intake during adjustment. Bring familiar food—local options may differ. Sun protection for light-colored or short-haired breeds recommended.
