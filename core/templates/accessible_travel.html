RWANDA FOR EVERYONE

Travel should be accessible to all. We specialize in creating Rwanda experiences for travelers with mobility challenges, visual or hearing impairments, and other accessibility needs—ensuring everyone can explore Africa's most beautiful country comfortably and safely.

Your Accessibility Needs, Our Priority

We believe Rwanda's wonders should be accessible to everyone, regardless of physical abilities. While infrastructure is still developing in some areas, many experiences can be fully adapted—and we know exactly how to make it happen.

From wheelchair-accessible game drives to sensory-focused cultural experiences, we customize every aspect of your journey to ensure comfort, safety, and full participation in Rwanda's magic.

Comprehensive Accessibility Support

We provide end-to-end support for travelers with diverse accessibility needs. Here's how we ensure your Rwanda journey is comfortable, safe, and deeply rewarding.

♿

Mobility Support

Wheelchair-accessible accommodations, adapted vehicles with lifts or easy entry, portable ramps, and personal assistance throughout your journey.

👁️

Visual Assistance

Sighted guides, tactile experiences, audio descriptions, and adapted formats ensure travelers with visual impairments fully experience Rwanda's culture and nature.

👂

Hearing Support

Sign language interpreters (arranged in advance), written materials, visual aids, and hearing loop systems at select venues for travelers with hearing impairments.

🏨 Accessible Accommodations

We personally inspect properties to verify accessibility features. Not all lodges meet international standards, but we've identified those that do—and we're transparent about what's available.

  • Ground-floor or elevator-accessible rooms in Kigali
  • Roll-in showers at select modern properties
  • Ramps and wide doorways where available
  • Accessible bathroom grab bars and fixtures
  • Alert systems for hearing-impaired guests
  • Braille signage at newer facilities

🚐 Adapted Transportation

We arrange vehicles specifically equipped for your needs, whether that's hydraulic lifts for wheelchairs or extra space for mobility aids.

  • Vehicles with hydraulic wheelchair lifts (advance booking required)
  • Low-entry vans for easier boarding
  • Portable ramps for standard vehicles
  • Extra space for wheelchairs and mobility aids
  • Driver assistance with boarding and transfers
  • Frequent rest stops and comfort breaks

🏥 Healthcare Coordination

Kigali has excellent private medical facilities with English-speaking doctors. We coordinate all healthcare needs before and during your trip.

  • Pre-arrange medical appointments if needed
  • Wheelchair and mobility aid rentals
  • Prescription medication refills and pharmacy access
  • Oxygen and specialized medical equipment coordination
  • 24/7 medical emergency contacts and support
  • Travel insurance guidance and recommendations

👥 Personal Assistance

Personal care assistants, companions, and support staff can be arranged with sufficient advance notice to help with daily activities.

  • Personal care assistants available
  • Professional caregivers for complex medical needs
  • Sign language interpreters (book 4+ weeks ahead)
  • Sighted guides for visual impairment
  • Companion assistance for solo travelers
  • All assistants are background-checked professionals

💡 Honest Accessibility Assessment

We're transparent about Rwanda's accessibility reality: Kigali has good infrastructure, but rural areas can be challenging. We'll never promise accessibility we can't deliver—instead, we focus on what IS possible and help you experience Rwanda safely within those realities.

Accessible gallery visits with tactile art experiences available

🚫 What's Not Accessible

We're upfront about limitations. Gorilla trekking requires significant hiking on steep, uneven terrain. Some remote lodges lack accessibility features. Rural areas have limited infrastructure.

However, for every inaccessible experience, we offer compelling alternatives that deliver similar rewards without the physical demands.

✨ What We Can Adapt

Many experiences become accessible with proper planning. Golden monkey tracking (less strenuous), cultural center visits, wildlife viewing from vehicles, and lakeside relaxation all work beautifully.

Tell us your specific needs and interests—we'll design an itinerary that maximizes what you CAN do rather than limiting you by what you can't.
