Culinary Adventures

FOOD TOURS

Discover Rwanda's rich culinary heritage through guided tastings of traditional dishes, street food adventures, and farm-to-table experiences that tell the story of this vibrant culture.

Signature Dishes

Ugali & Isombe

Staple cornmeal porridge served with cassava leaves in peanut sauce—a beloved combination that represents Rwandan comfort food at its finest.

Traditional Vegetarian

Brochettes

Grilled meat skewers seasoned with African spices, served with fried plantains—Rwanda's favorite street food found in markets across the country.

Street Food Grilled

Fresh Lake Tilapia

Whole grilled fish from Lake Kivu, served with traditional sides—a lakeside specialty that showcases Rwanda's freshwater abundance.

Seafood Lake Kivu

Matoke

Steamed plantains in banana leaves—a traditional preparation method that infuses the dish with subtle herbal notes and authentic Rwandan flavors.
