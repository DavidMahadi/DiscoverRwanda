Kwita Izina — September
Annual gorilla naming ceremony at Volcanoes National Park. Traditional performances, conservation celebration.
Intore Dance
Traditional royal court dance. Nightly performances at Iwacu Cultural Village near Kinigi.
Kigali Up Festival — July
East Africa's leading music festival. Afrobeats, jazz, hip-hop, and traditional fusion.
Kigali Marathon — January
International road race through the city's rolling hills. Open to all abilities.
